Delete Blank Lines and Replace With Enter

Nov 5th, 2012

How to replace , with enter

e.g. How to replace aaa,bbb,ccc with

aaa
bbb
ccc

open it with mvim

:%s/,/^M/g
# you need to type CTRL-V <CR> to get a ^M here

How to remove blank lines

:%g/^\s*$/d
